🎯 Understanding the Passive Voice in English

The passive voice is used when the focus is on the action, not who is doing it. The structure includes a form of to be + the past participle of the main verb.

🗣 Tounsi: Passive voice tist3mel ki n7ebbou nkounou focus 3la l-fi3l, moch 3la chkon 3melou. Structure: to be (fel tense ili 7achtek bih) + past participle (verbe fel passé participe).

🧱 Passive Voice Structure by Tense

  • Present Simple: am / is / are + past participle
    Ex: The homework is done every day.
    Ex: The door is closed at 6 PM.
  • Past Simple: was / were + past participle
    Ex: The cake was baked yesterday.
    Ex: The email was sent an hour ago.
  • Future Simple: will be + past participle
    Ex: The house will be cleaned tomorrow.
    Ex: The documents will be delivered soon.
  • Present Continuous: am / is / are being + past participle
    Ex: The walls are being painted now.
    Ex: A new road is being built.
  • Past Perfect: had been + past participle
    Ex: The project had been finished before the deadline.
    Ex: The letter had been sent before he called.
  • Future Perfect: will have been + past participle
    Ex: The task will have been completed by 5 PM.
    Ex: All files will have been backed up before the update.

🔍 Recognizing the Passive

Look for the "to be" verb and a past participle:

  • The door was opened.
  • The books are sold online.
  • Lunch is being prepared.
  • The package has been delivered.

Tounsi: Yji "be" + verbe fi passé. Matlawech 3la "who", l’action hiya l’important.

📌 When Do We Use Passive?

  • The doer is unknown: My phone was stolen.
  • To sound formal: The report will be submitted soon.
  • To emphasize the action: A new law was passed.
  • For instructions: You are requested to sign here.
  • In news: A building was destroyed in the fire.

❌ Passive Voice Errors & Corrections

  • ❌ The book was write by her.
    ✅ The book was written by her.
  • ❌ The game is play now.
    ✅ The game is being played now.
  • ❌ They were being build a house.
    ✅ A house was being built.

🗒 Practice – Passive Voice Exercises

  1. Transform to passive:
    They deliver the mail every morning.
    ✅ The mail is delivered every morning.
  2. Correct the mistake:
    The car was drive by John.
    ✅ The car was driven by John.
  3. Fill in the blank:
    A new hospital ______ (build) next year.
    ✅ will be built
  4. Change to passive:
    She writes emails every day.
    ✅ Emails are written every day.
  5. Correct the mistake:
    The song is sing by a famous artist.
    ✅ The song is sung by a famous artist.
  6. Fill in the blank:
    The letters ______ (send) yesterday.
    ✅ were sent
